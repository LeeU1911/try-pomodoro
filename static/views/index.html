How it works?
1. At the beginning of the day, list out your tasks in priority from high to low
2. Work on tasks (from the top) for a Pomodoro (25 minutes) without distractions (calls, messages, gossip, etc)
3. When 25 minutes is up, a Pomodoro is recorded (as an X)
4. Take a 5-minute short-break, then continue with another Pomodoro
5. Every 4 Pomodoros, take a 15-minute long-break
